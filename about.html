About us

The Sisavan winery is located in the village of Sisavan, Ararat province, in the heart of the Ararat Valley. The Ararat Valley is considered the most ancient cradle of viticulture and winemaking. During excavations in Van, Urartian jars were found, which serve as graves for the burial of the Van fortress, as well as basins where grapes were crushed to produce wine.


According to the Bible,Noah planted the first vine in the Ararat Valley. Since then, the Armenian people have been engaged in winemaking in the Ararat Valley. Continuing the tradition, it was decided to build a factory right on this territory.Thus, until 2015, the company had seriously prepared for entering the market and has so far taken the necessary steps in this direction: it has built production buildings, workshops, warehouses, and acquired the latest technologies: Today, the management of the organization can clearly say that the winery "Sisavan" is fully ready to enter the market and take its special place there. Since the beginning of 2015, the company has started to carry out this mission.


The organization already has successful and established distributors in several countries with specific “Sisavan” and “Private Lebel” branding. The winery "Sisavan" will attract the lover of wine and cognac with its unique products.The products of the winery "Sisavan" have a number of advantages.specialists with appropriate professional education and qualifications. availability of conditions for the implementation of the necessary technological processes and the consistent implementation of these conditions, favorable working conditions, high responsibility of the management and the workforce to the cause, dedication and hard work, daily updates and improvement of technologies, equipment and working conditions, and, based on all this, high quality.
